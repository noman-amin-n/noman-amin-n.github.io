Noman Amin

Lecturer
Department of CSE, Southeast University

Noman Amin is a Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Southeast University, Bangladesh. His research focuses on video coding and compression, transformer-driven acceleration for VVC, and attention-based computer vision. He earned his B.Sc. in CSE from Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology (RUET), graduating in the top 4% of his class.

Areas of Expertise

01. Video Coding and Compression

Focus on complexity reduction in VVC using Vision Transformers and deep convolutional networks to achieve a strong rate-distortion balance.

02. Applied Deep Learning

Designing and training tailored models (DenseNet, ConvNeXt) for critical tasks like skin lesion detection and deepfake mitigation in competitive environments.

03. Digital Systems Design

Expertise in low-level hardware design (Verilog, OpenLane, Skywater 130 nm PDK).
